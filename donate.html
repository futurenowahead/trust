Donation

Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam Trust, Kollukkadu, warmly welcomes the public to participate in carrying out the various spiritual and service activities envisioned by Sri Agathiyar Siddhar for the welfare of humanity. Those who wish to offer their voluntary services may kindly contact the Math Office. Admirers and devotees who support the ideals of the Trust but are unable to participate personally are cordially invited to extend their support by contributing to one or more of the Trust’s Funds, which require their active help.


Devotees who wish to perform poojas on their preferred dates may send any amount they wish by Demand Draft addressed to the Temple Trustee, Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam Trust,Kollukkadu.

The best offering is the one made with sincere devotion. If you wish to contribute, please donate whatever amount you can and mention your intentions or wishes. The poojas will be performed with full devotion, and the prasadam (holy offerings) will be sent to you immediately with Sri Agathiyar Siddhar’s blessings. Any shortfall in the required amount will be covered by the Trust.
