History

"Agathiyar Siddhar at Kollukkadu"

Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam Trust, located at Pattukkottai Union, Thanjavur District, Tamil Nadu, is a sacred spiritual center established with deep devotion to Sage Agathiyar, the foremost among the 18 Tamil Siddhars. The Peedam stands as a divine seat where meditation, prayer, healing, and service come together to uplift the lives of devotees seeking peace, wisdom, and spiritual guidance. Rooted in the holy vibrations of Siddhar blessings, this Peedam serves as a living lighthouse of divine grace. It is believed that the sanctity of this place has been strengthened by the unseen presence and blessings of Siddhars, saints, and spiritual beings who have walked this sacred land. Agathiyar, revered for his unmatched wisdom, humility, and compassion, continues to inspire seekers through his silent but powerful presence here.


"Our Spiritual Mission"


Established through divine inspiration, Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam Trust – Thanjavur functions with the objective of spreading Siddha wisdom and supporting the community through spiritual and humanitarian services. The Trust follows the ancient Siddhar path — the path of simplicity, discipline, truth, and direct realization of the Divine.
At the Peedam, the following activities are carried out for the welfare of devotees and society:


  • Meditation & Yogic Practices
  • Teachings on Siddha Philosophy & Spirituality
  • Awareness on Herbal Healing & Traditional Remedies
  • Distribution of Annadhanam (Free Food Offering)
  • Support for Poor & Needy Families
  • Regular Poojas, Homams & Rituals Dedicated to Agathiyar

"A Place of Divine Guidance"


Devotees from across Tamil Nadu and from various parts of the world visit the Peedam to experience the serene and healing vibrations of Sage Agathiyar. Many believe that sincere prayers offered here bring protection, spiritual progress, and life transformation.
The Peedam embodies the essence of the Siddhar tradition — a tradition that encourages:


  • Living with humility
  • Serving with compassion
  • Walking the path of truth
  • Seeking the Divine within
  • Support for Poor & Needy Families

"Our Ongoing Service"


Through the continuous efforts of our trustees, volunteers, and devotees, Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam Trust – Thanjavur remains dedicated to preserving the divine legacy of Sage Agathiyar for future generations. What began as a humble spiritual space has now grown into a revered center of light, devotion, and healing. The Trust stands steadfast in its mission to bring the eternal message of Agathiyar Siddhar — Love, Truth, and Divine Wisdom — into the hearts of all seekers.
