About Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam Trust

This website is lovingly dedicated to Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam Trust, a spiritual centre established to honor the divine wisdom, grace, and eternal presence of Sage Agathiyar, the foremost of Siddhars. Sage Agathiyar, revered as the embodiment of divine knowledge and cosmic balance, is believed to have descended to Earth with a sacred purpose — a mission beyond human comprehension, yet one that can be profoundly experienced through his blessings and teachings. His compassionate grace continues to uplift devotees, guiding them toward peace, clarity, spiritual awakening, and liberation.

The Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam Trust strives to preserve, practice, and propagate the timeless teachings and rituals associated with Agathiyar Siddhar. Through spiritual gatherings, charitable activities, traditional ceremonies, publications, and community welfare initiatives, the Trust serves as a sacred bridge connecting people to the divine path shown by Agathiyar.

Agathiyar Siddhar Peedam

Sage Agasthiyar Siddhar attained Mahasamadhi at Kollukkadu, leaving behind a legacy of wisdom, healing, and spiritual guidance. Even after his samadhi, devotees have often felt a divine presence and an aura of peace at the Peedam. When his sacred rites were performed with due Vedic rituals, extraordinary signs of divinity were witnessed — the sky, which had been calm, became solemn and sanctified the occasion with a gentle shower over the Peedam, while birds, considered as divine messengers, circled reverently above the sacred site. These miraculous events are cherished by devotees as a testament to the Siddhar’s eternal grace and spiritual power.
