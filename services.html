Our Services

Service Description
Food for Poor People 		Daily Annadhanam (Free Food Service) is conducted to support the needy. Devotees contribute groceries, rice, vegetables, and funds to ensure that no one in the village suffers from hunger.
Education for Poor People 		The Trust provides school kits, uniforms, books, and basic study materials for underprivileged rural children. Scholarships and academic support are also offered to deserving students.
Medical & Welfare Support 		Free medical camps, herbal awareness programs, and health assistance are arranged for the poor. The Trust also supports families in need during emergencies.
Meditation & Yogic Practices 		Daily and weekly meditation sessions are conducted for devotees to attain mental peace, emotional stability, and spiritual awakening. Traditional yogic practices taught here help individuals increase focus, discipline, and inner strength.
Siddha Philosophy & Herbal Awareness 		The Trust spreads awareness on Siddha medicine, herbal healing, and traditional remedies. Programs are organized to educate people about natural herbs, ancient healing methods, and holistic wellness practices followed by Siddhars.
Spiritual Guidance & Counseling 		Devotees receive personal spiritual guidance based on Siddhar teachings to overcome life struggles. Counseling sessions help seekers find clarity, faith, and inner strength through Agathiyar’s divine path.
Temple Rituals & Special Poojas 		The Peedam conducts regular poojas, homams, and festive rituals dedicated to Sage Agathiyar. Special occasions such as Siddhar Guru Poojas and spiritual festivals are celebrated with devotion for the welfare of devotees and the community.
